## Rules applied

- Exact 32×32 four-node/three-edge Graph of Life glyph, with edges drawn before nodes.
- IBM Plex Sans for prose and IBM Plex Mono for numbers, units, provenance, dates, controls, and source identifiers.
- Exact canonical palette. Teal carries connection, green supported synthetic signal, amber caution/indicative evidence, and coral risk/blocked/abstention.
- `#06141F` is used only as the sanctioned instrument-mode dark translation documented by the reference implementation.
- Sparse seeded network texture stays below the reading layer and carries no essential data.
- Sticky full-viewport chapters, restrained copy cards, fixed claim boundary, source pills, and no essential canvas-only content.
