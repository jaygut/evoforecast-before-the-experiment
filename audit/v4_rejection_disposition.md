## Disposition

The fourth blind cohort (V4) was reviewed and **rejected**. Its numerical estimates
are not scientific evidence and are carried into no later conclusion. The V5 result
was produced by a new cohort, not by repairing V4 in place.

## Defects found

- The release exposed information a forecaster should not have seen (hidden-state leakage).
- Paired comparisons no longer matched the registered scoring unit (broken pairing and resampling).
- Policy regret and the experiment portfolio were not internally coherent.
- Cost and uncertainty could not support a decision.

## Consequence

- No V4 estimate appears in the V5 conclusion or in any facts-digest claim value.
- The rejection is presented in the story (beat V4) as evidence that the review can stop a
  bad result before it reaches a decision.
