What the latest challenge says

The genome-plus-phenome track, combining genome data with measured traits, improved its mean continuous ranked probability score, or CRPS, by 47.5 percent over the pedigree baseline, aggregated across 35 registered contexts. CRPS measures forecast accuracy. Its ninety percent prediction range held the truth eight times in ten. Zero forward-looking cells reached the combined bar. Average accuracy, calibration, population-level identifiability, and transfer are separate tests.

Interpretation. This is informative synthetic design evidence. Biological forecast skill awaits an empirical round.

Why controls matter

A corrected favorable marker control produced a 17.0 percent genome-versus-pedigree gain. A matched adverse control produced minus 9.2 percent. The opposite signs show the workflow can expose failure and avoid manufacturing a genomic advantage.

Supported claim. The software control behaves differently under registered favorable and adverse conditions.

One trajectory cell, fully exposed

Each adaptive_E07 vessel begins with 80 age-synchronized grazers, ten from each of eight immutable founders. All 32 retained grazers through day 90. Standing selection still produced different founder and population histories. This is a registered synthetic persistence diagnostic, and its value lies in the distribution that practitioners would need to forecast.

What remains blocked

Empirical qualification requires exact material identity, accepted starting measurements, real assay performance, material custody, independent prospective reveal, uncertainty analysis, safety authority, and external reproduction. Diploid sexual genetics and ecological network dynamics remain future directions.

Run one small round together.

Bring one candidate keystone organism, one contained stressor, one feasible budget stated in USD, and one outcome sealed until scoring. We rehearse the design, then run the smallest real round that can estimate calibration and forecast accuracy under an independent reveal. A later contained round can test whether a targeted intervention improves resilience after the forecast layer earns trust.

EvoForecast turns prediction into a testable programme decision. Measure what can discriminate, seal the outcome, score the forecast, and scale only after the evidence earns it.
